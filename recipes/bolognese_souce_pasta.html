Bolognese Souce Pasta

Authentic Bolognese Souce, or Ragu alla Bolognese, a taste of Italy

This delicious Authentic Bolognese Sauce or Ragu alla Bolognese is made with fresh ingredients and cooked low and slow. A true Bolognese sauce recipe takes time, but it is so worth it. For the true Italian tradition serve with fresh egg Tagliatelle or Pappardelle!

Ingredients

  • Extra Vergin Olive oil
  • Carrot
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Ground Beef
  • Bacon
  • Red Wine
  • Tomato Puree
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Start by chopping finely the bacon
  2. Then in a medium to large pot add the olive oil and the bacon and leave it at low heat
  3. Now chop the carrot, celery and onion very finely, but not too fine that it turns into a pulp.
  4. Add the vegetables to the bacon and let it cook until the onion is transparent
  5. Raise the heat to medium and add the ground beef and pork, stirring and breaking up the meat as it cooks and browns.
  6. Raise the heat to high and add the red wine and cook until the wine has evaporated. The liquid should evaporate without burning the meat or veggies.
  7. Lower the heat to medium and add the Tomato Puree
  8. Add salt and pepper
  9. Bring the souce to a boil stirring from time to time
  10. Then lower the heat to the minimum, cover the pot but leaving a slight opening.
  11. Now let the souce cook for 2 to 3 hours, stirring occasionally
  12. Serve over cooked pasta
